About Parts Identification

The parts identification process will:

  • Scan all assessment sections for damaged components
  • Create individual records for each damaged part
  • Estimate labor hours based on damage severity
  • Link damage photos to specific parts
  • Prepare parts for quote collection from providers

This process enables accurate, itemized quotes from multiple providers instead of using generic cost estimates.
