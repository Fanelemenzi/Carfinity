Financial Assessment Guidelines

Valuation Guidelines

  • Repair Cost: Include all parts, labor, and associated costs
  • Market Value: Use current market prices for similar vehicles
  • Salvage Value: Consider parts value and scrap metal value

Decision Rules

  • 70% Rule: If repair costs exceed 70% of market value, consider total loss
  • Economic Total Loss: When repair cost + salvage value > market value
  • Consider: Hidden damage, depreciation, and safety factors
