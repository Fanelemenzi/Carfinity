📸 Why Document Your Work?

Adding photos helps create a complete maintenance record, provides proof of work completed, and assists future technicians who may work on this vehicle.

📋 Best Practices:
  • • Take clear, well-lit photos
  • • Show before/after conditions
  • • Include part numbers when visible
  • • Document any damage found
🔧 What to Photograph:
  • • Replaced parts and components
  • • Work area before starting
  • • Completed repair/service
  • • Any issues discovered

💡 Pro Tip: Good documentation protects both you and the customer by providing clear evidence of professional work completed according to standards.
